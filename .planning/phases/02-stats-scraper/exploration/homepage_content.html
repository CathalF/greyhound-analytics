Welcome to the Greyhound Stats UK website


Welcome to the Greyhound Stats UK website where we believe you will find the widest range of data and statistics available to British greyhound racing enthusiasts.

The information on the site is based on that posted on official results sites and whilst we do the undertake checks of the input it is inevitable, given the volume of data, that errors will occur and if you spot any we've missed please let us know so that they can be corrected.

Our aim is simply to provide all of the information which may be helpful to followers of our sport whatever their particular interest - it is then for you to decide how best to use it.

We are always looking to improve the site and if there is anything you'd like to see added then please let us know.
